About

The founder of BMS Online Institute is Dr. Maxwell Balla Nvani, a Dentist. The current principal, Vice principal - admin and Vice principal - Academics of the school would also be displayed. The school comprises of a 2 year program. First year is targeted at conventional SS1 curriculum and part of SS2 curriculum. Second year consist of the remainder of SS2 curriculum and SS3 curriculum. Subjects would span both arts and science. Students also have the option of choosing one skill to learn ranging from Baking, Sewing, Kneating, Shoe making, etc. Each day is structured to start from 8am to 4pm excluding saturdays and sundays. There would be morning gathering sessions between students in a class and an instructor which would be a live video session. Here, students would have the opportunity to talk about anything bothering him/her. They would also be emotionally and spiritually encouraged. Each day asides saturday and sunday, one major subject would be taught with an accompanying post-lecture test since each student would have 5 major courses to concentrate on. Minor subjects would also be taught appropriately. There would be end of week assessments and end of term (after 3 months) examination. Each student would be provided with a customised tablet device. Parents would be emailed the perfomance of his/her child including attendance to gathering and classes, post-lecture tests and end of term exams. Saturdays, from 7am to 8am would be for sporting activities. Please note that the manner of teaching is via customised videos with enough and elaborate demonstrations, explanations and diagrams. These videos are made by qualified persons with degrees in the respective fields. Students would be responsible for registering for the WAEC NOV-DEC Exams. However, they would be guided. There would also be room for a JAN-FEB exam if they do not pass the NOV-DEC exams. We also offer A-level curriculum for 3 major courses. Students would be responsible for traveling to the designated exam centres for the examinations. Our fee structure is flexible. Parents have the option of paying 6-monthly. Also, we have endeavoured to make this school very affordable and convenient. You don’t need to worry about security or transportation cost as your children would be taught from home.
